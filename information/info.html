Satellite

Deep Space Climate Observatory is a NOAA Earth observation and climate satellite launched by SpaceX on a Falcon 9 launch vehicle on February 11, 2015 from Cape Canaveral. It was originally developed as a NASA satellite, proposed in 1998 by then US Vice President.

Recurrent Neural Network (RNN)

The Recurrent Neural Network (RNN) is a model architecture proposed to face the Markov chain dependance. This architecture involves a recurrent function named “hidden state”, that is responsible for saving the data from previous time steps into the future time steps, thus leading to better dependency captions and prediction accuracy.

Geomagnetic Storms

A geomagnetic storm, also called a solar storm, is a temporary disturbance of the Earth's magnetosphere that can be caused by a solar wind shock wave and/or a coronal mass ejection interacting with the Earth's magnetic field.

Transformers

This architecture is the base of all text generative AI, BERT, GPT-2, GPT-3, all very well known. This approach involved very deep encoder-decoder architectures with a new way to approach sequential modeling, the attention mechanism, that can adaptively match vectors of one sequence into the output dimension.

Northern Lights

Northern Lights are a product of solar mass ejections, this way, the more Northern Lights we can see in the sky, the stronger the solar storm is. Our project intends to imitate this phenomena, as we too aim to develop a successful forecasting method for geomagnetic storms.
