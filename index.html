The Challenge

The Deep Space Climate Observatory (DSCOVR) enables us to predict geomagnetic storms that can severely impact important systems like GPS and electrical power grids on Earth. Developing novel methods for geomagnetic forecasting with raw data from DSCOVR and publishing the results for the open science comunity.
